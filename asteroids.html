Asteroids

INSTRUCTIONS
1.Press or hold w key to move the ship.
2.Move or rotate the mouse to control the movement direction.
3.Press and release left mouse button to shoot bullets to destroy asteroids, don't hold the mouse, it will not work.
4.You only have 3 lives before the ship is destroyed, use it wisely!
5. You can wrap around the map by going through the 4 edges of the map and the ship will come out from the other side.
